Types of SEO

SEO is divided into three main categories based on where and how optimization takes place. Understanding each type helps you build a complete SEO strategy.

1. On-Page SEO

On-page SEO means optimizing the content and HTML elements inside your website. These are things you have direct control over.

  • Title tag optimization
  • Meta descriptions
  • Heading structure (H1, H2, H3)
  • Keyword placement
  • Internal links

2. Off-Page SEO

Off-page SEO means improving your website's authority and reputation through actions outside your site.

  • Backlinks from other websites
  • Social media presence
  • Guest posting
  • Brand mentions

3. Technical SEO

Technical SEO means improving the backend structure and performance of your website so search engines can crawl and index it efficiently.

  • Website loading speed
  • Mobile-friendliness
  • XML sitemap
  • robots.txt configuration
  • Secure HTTPS connection
