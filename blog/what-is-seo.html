What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of improving a website so that it appears higher in search engine results pages (SERPs) like Google, Bing, or Yahoo.

When someone searches for a topic online, search engines display a list of results. SEO helps your website show up near the top of those results — without paying for ads.

Why SEO Is Important

SEO helps websites get free, organic traffic. Without SEO, even a great website may be invisible to people searching for its content. Higher rankings mean more visitors, more trust, and more opportunities.

How SEO Works

Search engines follow a three-step process to decide what content to show:

  • Crawling — Search engine bots visit your website and read its content.
  • Indexing — Your pages are stored in the search engine's database.
  • Ranking — The engine decides which pages to show for a given search query.

Types of SEO

There are three main types of SEO you should know:

  • On-page SEO — Optimizing content, headings, and meta tags on your pages.
  • Off-page SEO — Building authority through backlinks and social signals.
  • Technical SEO — Improving site speed, structure, and crawlability.
